Problem Statement

Traditional workspace models fail to meet the needs of modern professionals, creating significant inefficiencies:

Space Inefficiency

40% of commercial space sits empty during business hours(JLL Research, 2023)

High Costs

Lack of Flexibility

82% of freelancers report difficulty finding affordable, professional workspaces(FlexJobs Survey, 2023)

Current Solutions Fall Short

  • Traditional co-working spaces require long-term commitments and are often centrally located, increasing commute times

  • Cafés and home offices lack professional infrastructure and networking opportunities
  • Existing solutions don't effectively utilize the 15M+ sq ft of underutilized commercial space in Bengaluru alone(Knight Frank Research)

Deciding Factors
